# Begging

When tolls, licences and gates have bled your purse dry, SexLab Survival gives a woman at the bottom a way to keep going: begging for coin in the streets. It lives on the **Begging & Kennel** MCM page.

## Begging

A broke, unlicensed woman can **beg for coin** in cities. Begging is a small minigame / activity: you take up a spot and work passers-by for charity, earning modest gold "drops" over time rather than a lump sum.

It is not a living wage. It is a floor — a way to scrape together enough coin to cover a toll or the cheapest [licence](licences.md) when you have no other income. When the survival economy has stripped you to nothing, begging is the pressure valve that keeps you from being completely stuck.

!!! tip "Plugging the gold economy"
    SLS's tolls, evictions and licence fees are designed to drain gold faster than casual play refills it — see [Tolls, Eviction & Gates](tolls-eviction-gates.md). Begging exists so that "I have zero coin and can't pay the gate" is a hardship you can claw out of, not a dead end.

The per-drop amount and how readily citizens give are configurable in the MCM. Expect coppers, not fortunes.

## Other options

The **Begging & Kennel** page also holds additional, harder-edged optional mechanics tied to confinement and slavery-themed content. These are off or configurable in the MCM and are not detailed here — enable only what suits your playthrough.

!!! note
    Begging and the page's other options are all configured on the **Begging & Kennel** MCM page.
