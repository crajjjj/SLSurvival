# Sex, Trauma & Effects

Sex in SexLab Survival isn't a free action with no consequences. It leaves marks — on your stats, your body, your mind, and your urges. This page covers the **Sex & Effects** and **Trauma** MCM pages: the systems that turn each encounter into something that follows you afterward.

## Sex Experience & Effects

With **Enable Sex Experience** on, SLS tracks sex stats that grow over time. The more you do, the more experienced your character becomes, and that experience feeds back into how encounters play out. It's a persistent record rather than a one-off flag — a long-lived character accumulates a history the game can react to.

## Corruption

Some partners corrupt you. **Creature Corruption** and **Dremora Orgasms Corrupt** feed a corruption meter that climbs as you have sex with creatures or daedra. It's a slow slide meant to represent a character being changed by what she does. Leave the corruption toggles off if you want creature and daedra encounters to carry no lasting taint.

## Ahegao

**Ahegao** puts a facial expression on your character at the moment of orgasm — a visible, involuntary tell. **Duration Per Orgasm** controls how long the expression lingers before your face settles back. It's a purely cosmetic layer, easily disabled if it's not to taste.

## Jiggles

**Jiggles** drives body-scaling visual effects through SLIF (SexLab Inflation Framework), scaling breasts, belly and ass in response to what's happening to your character. **SLIF Max Scaling** caps how far those inflation effects can push, so you can keep the look within whatever range you find reasonable.

## Compulsive Sex

Sex can become a need in its own right. **Compulsive Sex** builds an urge over time, governed by **Time Between Fucks** — the longer you go without, the stronger the compulsion pushing you back toward it. It turns abstinence into its own kind of pressure rather than a neutral choice.

## Enjoyment & rape mechanics

- **Cock Size Bonus Enjoyment** ties how much your character gets out of an encounter to her partner's size, adding a bit of variety to how different encounters land.
- **Minimum Rapist Arousal** sets the arousal threshold an NPC must cross before non-consensual events become possible — a gate on when the world can turn on you.
- **Rape Forced Drug Chance** governs the likelihood of being forcibly drugged during such an event, another way an assault can leave a lasting hook.

## Trauma

The **Trauma** page models the psychological after-effects of assault. Rather than an encounter ending cleanly, it can leave your character shaken, with knock-on effects that persist afterward. It's the mental counterpart to the physical marks the rest of these systems leave — and, like the rest, something you can dial down or off if you'd rather keep encounters consequence-light.

See also [Cum & Bukkake](cum.md) for the visible residue an encounter leaves and the social fallout that follows.

!!! note
    Everything here lives on the **Sex & Effects** and **Trauma** MCM pages. Each system — experience, corruption, ahegao, jiggles, compulsion, the rape gates, and trauma — can be tuned or disabled individually there.
