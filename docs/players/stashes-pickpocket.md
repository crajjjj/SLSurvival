# Stashes & Pickpocket

This page covers two related MCM pages: **Stashes**, and **Pickpocket & Dismemberment**. Together they deal with hiding what you own and the risks of taking what you don't.

## Stashes

Guards confiscate gear from unlicensed women (see [Licences](licences.md)), so anything valuable you carry into town is at risk. **Stashes** are hidden caches where you can tuck gear away before you walk into that risk — a place to leave weapons, armor or contraband you don't want taken at a checkpoint, then reclaim later.

Use stashes as a rhythm: cache the gear you can't legally carry, enter town light, retrieve it on the way out. Where stashes can be placed and how they behave is configurable on the **Stashes** MCM page.

## Pickpocket

SLS modifies vanilla pickpocketing rules. Devious effects change what your hands can do: when you are restrained or gagged, the **Pickpocketing Is** setting can make theft **difficult** or outright **impossible**. A woman in bondage simply can't work a mark's pockets the way an unbound thief can.

This is set on the **Pickpocket & Dismemberment** page — tune how much your restraints interfere with pickpocketing, or turn the restriction off.

## Other options

The **Pickpocket & Dismemberment** page also holds an optional hardcore punishment mechanic. It is off by default and configurable in the MCM; it is not detailed here — review the in-game page and enable only what suits your playthrough.

!!! note
    Stashes are configured on the **Stashes** MCM page; pickpocket rules and the page's other options live on the **Pickpocket & Dismemberment** MCM page.
