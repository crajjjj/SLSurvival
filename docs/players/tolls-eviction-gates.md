# Tolls, Eviction & Gates

This system puts a price on the things you normally do for free: **entering a city**, **keeping a roof over your head**, and **owning a home**. Together they turn movement and shelter into recurring costs that a poor character has to plan around.

## City-gate tolls

Every walled city can charge a **toll at the gate**. Approach, and you pay to be let in — in **gold**, or, if you cannot, in other ways. Tolls make travel between cities a deliberate choice rather than a free stroll: hop between holds too casually and the coin drains away.

Because [gold has weight](needs.md) in SLS and coin is hard to come by, tolls press directly on the same purse you fill through [begging](begging-kennel.md) and the odd job. A broke character can find herself locked out of the very cities where she might earn money — which is the point.

!!! tip "Plan your route"
    Tolls make the cheapest path between two places matter. Consolidate errands into fewer city visits instead of passing gates repeatedly.

## Inn eviction & room prices

Rooms are no longer a one-off cost. SLS can **evict** you from an inn room and enforce ongoing **inn room prices**, so shelter becomes a running expense. Fall behind and you are back on the street. The nightly rates are set on the dedicated **Inn Room Prices** MCM page.

## Home options & prices

The vanilla player homes can be made available on softer terms — the "Easy" home options — each with its own configurable price:

| Home | City |
|------|------|
| **Easy Breezehome** | Whiterun |
| **Easy Proudspire Manor** | Solitude |
| **Easy Vlindrel Hall** | Markarth |
| **Easy Hjerim** | Windhelm |
| **Easy Honeyside** | Riften |

These let you acquire a home under SLS's rules and pricing rather than the vanilla quests, giving you a fixed base to escape gate tolls and inn rent — once you can afford the buy-in.

!!! warning "It all draws on one purse"
    Gate tolls, inn rent and home prices compete for the same limited, *weighty* gold as [licences](licences.md) and needs. Budget across all of them — clearing one cost by emptying your purse can leave you stranded at the next gate.

## Configuring

Tolls, eviction and the home options live on the **Tolls, Eviction & Gates** MCM page, with nightly rates on the separate **Inn Room Prices** page. Toll amounts, whether eviction is enabled, and each home's price are all adjustable there. Turn any part down or off on those pages if you want a gentler economy without disabling the rest of the mod.
